‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the DataTalksClub Zoomcamp Projects Gallery?

It's a free, searchable database of 2000+ capstone projects submitted by students who completed DataTalksClub's online courses. Students use it to find inspiration for their own Data Engineering, ML, MLOps, and LLM projects.

How can I find Data Engineering Zoomcamp project ideas?

Visit the interactive dashboard, select "dezoomcamp" from the course filter, and browse hundreds of past student projects. You can filter by year, cloud provider (AWS, GCP, Azure), and deployment type (batch, streaming).

What technologies are used in these projects?

Projects commonly use Apache Airflow, Apache Kafka, Apache Spark, dbt, Docker, Terraform, Google BigQuery, AWS, GCP, Azure, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, MLflow, Prefect, and various LLM frameworks.

Is DataTalksClub free?

Yes, all DataTalksClub courses are completely free. They offer self-paced learning with cohort-based deadlines for capstone projects. Visit datatalks.club to enroll.
